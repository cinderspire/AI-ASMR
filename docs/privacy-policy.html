Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 5, 2024

Introduction

AI ASMR ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Favorites Data: Locally stored on your device
  • Theme Preferences: Dark/light mode selection stored locally
  • Sleep Timer Settings: Timer duration preferences stored locally

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • App Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app (locally stored only)
  • Network Status: Online/offline status for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the app
  • Improve and optimize app performance
  • Save your preferences locally
  • Enable offline functionality

Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT:

  • Transfer data to external servers
  • Store data in cloud databases
  • Share data with third parties
  • Collect personally identifiable information

Your Data Stays on Your Device

  • Favorites: Stored in local browser storage
  • Preferences: Stored in local browser storage
  • No account creation required
  • No data synchronization

Third-Party Services

Audio Content

Audio files are streamed from third-party content providers. When streaming audio:

  • Your IP address may be visible to CDN providers
  • Standard HTTP request headers are sent
  • No personally identifiable information is transmitted

Capacitor Plugins

We use the following Capacitor plugins (local device access only):

  • Haptics: Vibration feedback (device only)
  • Status Bar: UI customization (device only)
  • Splash Screen: App launch screen (device only)
  • Network: Online/offline detection (device only)

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally on the device and no accounts are created, children can use the app safely under parental supervision.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • All data stored locally on your device
  • No cloud storage or external databases
  • No data transmission to our servers
  • Standard HTTPS protocols for audio streaming

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete: Clear app data or uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Modify: Change preferences and favorites at any time
  • Export: Your data is in standard browser storage format

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Notifying users via in-app message (for significant changes)
