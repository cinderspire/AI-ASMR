# Privacy Policy for AI ASMR

**Last Updated:** November 5, 2024

## Introduction

AI ASMR ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Favorites Data**: Locally stored on your device
- **Theme Preferences**: Dark/light mode selection stored locally
- **Sleep Timer Settings**: Timer duration preferences stored locally

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version
- **App Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in app (locally stored only)
- **Network Status**: Online/offline status for app functionality

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain the app
- Improve and optimize app performance
- Save your preferences locally
- Enable offline functionality

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only
All user data is stored **locally on your device**. We do NOT:
- Transfer data to external servers
- Store data in cloud databases
- Share data with third parties
- Collect personally identifiable information

### Your Data Stays on Your Device
- Favorites: Stored in local browser storage
- Preferences: Stored in local browser storage
- No account creation required
- No data synchronization

## Third-Party Services

### Audio Content
Audio files are streamed from third-party CDN providers:
- SoundHelix (example content)
- Future: Licensed ASMR content providers

### Analytics (Future)
We may integrate analytics services in future versions:
- Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics
- Usage statistics (anonymized)
- Crash reporting

When implemented, we will update this policy and request your consent.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to maintain the security of your information:
- Local storage encryption (device-level)
- No transmission of personal data
- No user accounts or authentication
- No collection of sensitive information

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

If you are a European resident, you have the right to:
- **Access**: Request copies of your personal data (all stored locally)
- **Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data (clear app data on device)
- **Restriction**: Request restriction of processing
- **Portability**: Request transfer of your data (export favorites)
- **Object**: Object to our processing of your data

### How to Exercise Your Rights
Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- **View data**: Check app settings and favorites
- **Delete data**: Clear app data in Android settings or uninstall app
- **Export data**: Use browser developer tools to export local storage

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information:
- **Right to Know**: What personal information we collect (locally stored only)
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of personal information (clear app data)
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

## Cookies and Tracking

### Local Storage
We use browser local storage (not cookies) to save:
- User preferences (theme, favorites)
- App state

### No Tracking
We do NOT use:
- Tracking cookies
- Third-party tracking scripts
- Advertising identifiers
- Cross-site tracking

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice on first app launch after update
